Admissions

Start Your Child’s Journey at DXT Academy

We’re excited to welcome families seeking a school grounded in excellence, character, and confident preparation for the future.

How to Apply

A clear and supportive admissions process.

  • 1 Submit an enquiry or application form with your child’s details.
  • 2 Share academic records and complete the entrance assessment.
  • 3 Attend a family interaction and receive your admission decision.
  • 4 Confirm placement, complete enrolment, and join the DXT community.

Admission Requirements

  • ACompleted application form
  • BRecent passport photograph
  • CPrevious academic reports
  • DBirth certificate or valid identification
  • EEntrance assessment completion

Entry Windows

Admissions are open throughout the year, with major entry points aligned to the academic calendar and available spaces.

Family Guidance

Our admissions team supports parents with requirements, timelines, and next steps at every stage.

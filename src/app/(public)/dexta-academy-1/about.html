Our School Story

12+ Years of Building Excellence

DXT GRADE was founded with a clear and unwavering purpose: to create a learning environment where children receive strong academic preparation while also being known, guided, and inspired as individuals. From the first classroom to the vibrant school community we continue to build today, our focus has remained steadfast and true.

Our journey began with a simple belief that every child deserves a school experience that recognizes their unique strengths and helps them grow with confidence. We started small, but with great intention. What began as a handful of dedicated educators has evolved into a thriving institution, yet we have never lost sight of what made us special from day one: our commitment to knowing each child deeply.

Our Foundation

We believe every child carries unique potential within them. That is why our culture blends consistency, creativity, and character formation so learners can grow into capable, grounded young leaders. We don't just teach subjects; we cultivate curious minds, respectful hearts, and resilient spirits.

A British-Nigerian Education

Our British-Nigerian curriculum provides a robust academic foundation that prepares children for both local and international success. This balanced approach ensures our learners are competitive globally while remaining deeply rooted in their cultural identity and Nigerian heritage.

The Whole Child Approach

Academic excellence matters, but it is only part of the picture. At DXT GRADE, we develop character through discipline and integrity. We foster creativity through arts, music, and hands-on learning. We build confidence through sports, leadership opportunities, and a safe, nurturing environment where every child is valued.

Today, DXT GRADE stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Port Harcourt. Our alumni are thriving in secondary schools, universities, and beyond. Our current community of parents and learners continues to trust us with one of their most precious responsibilities: their child's growth and development.

As we look to the future, we remain committed to our founding vision. We will continue to be a school where unique potential is taken seriously, where every voice is heard, and where children discover not just what they can achieve, but who they can become.

Our Story

The School B story

School B was established in 2008 in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, with a clear vision: to offer quality secondary education in a setting that values discipline, personal growth, and long-term achievement.

From the beginning, the school was built around the belief that academic success and character formation should grow together. Students are encouraged to think clearly, work consistently, respect others, and develop the confidence to lead with integrity.

Today, School B serves junior and senior secondary learners in a structured day and boarding environment. Lessons, assessments, pastoral care, clubs, sports, and leadership opportunities all work together to support students as whole people, not just examination candidates.

Our classrooms, science and ICT spaces, routines, and co-curricular activities are designed to help learners discover their strengths while building discipline and responsibility. Every student is expected to pursue excellence, show respect, serve others, and prepare for meaningful life beyond school.

As the school continues to grow, our commitment remains steady: to raise confident, disciplined, and innovative learners who are ready to contribute positively in Nigeria and beyond.

How To Apply

Start Your Child’s DXT Academy Journey

We have made the application process simple for parents. Review what you need, submit the admission form, and our team will contact you with the next step.

Start Application ↗ Contact Admissions
01

Read The Requirements

Confirm the class or programme you want to apply for and prepare the basic student details.

02

Complete The Form

Fill the online admission form carefully using accurate parent, guardian, and student information.

03

Wait For Confirmation

Our admissions team reviews the submission and reaches out by phone or email.

04

Visit The School

Parents may be invited for a tour, discussion, or assessment depending on the child’s class level.

Have These Ready

  • Parent or guardian name, phone number, and email address
  • Child’s full name, age, and intended class
  • Previous school details, where applicable
  • Important health, learning, or support information

How to Apply

A clear admissions process designed to keep families informed at every step.

We want the application experience to feel organized and reassuring. Start with the online form, provide the requested student information, and our admissions team will guide you through the next stage.

1

Complete the online form

Fill in the admissions form below with your child's details and your preferred class entry point.

2

Prepare supporting records

Keep birth certificate, recent school result, passport photographs, and transfer records ready if applicable.

3

Wait for admissions follow-up

Our team will review the submission and contact you with the next steps, including assessment or interview details where required.

4

Complete enrollment

Successful applicants receive guidance on acceptance, fees, uniforms, and resumption arrangements.

Before You Begin

Keep these details nearby while completing the form.

  • Student full name, date of birth, and class applying into
  • Parent or guardian phone number and active email address
  • Last school attended and recent academic performance
  • Transfer note or testimonial for transfer applicants
Need help before submitting?

Call the admissions office or send an email if you need clarification about entry requirements, class availability, or supporting documents.
